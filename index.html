Core Verticals

LegalThings services all kinds of industries
Digital Transaction Management (DTM)
The end of the analog paper era is fast approaching and in an ever more digital world, expectations rise. Effective processes are crucial to keep up with these expectations.

LegalThings streamlines Digital Transaction Management by employing data enrichment accompanied with blockchain technology. This provides an opportunity to remove friction in transactions that are inherent to processes that involve people, data and documents. All of this while increasing your security and audit-ability.

LegalThings currently supplies:
  • Cradle-to-grave employment processes
  • Completely digitized real estate processes
  • Automation and digitization of incorporations
Regulatory and Compliance
Our world becomes increasingly interconnected. Globalization and digitization lead to more complex regulatory- and compliance requirements for corporations and governments.

LegalThings currently supplies:
  • GDPR Compliance for insurance companies
  • Automation of transfer pricing documentation
  • Workload reduction on small and petty crime handling
